Common Questions

Things you might wonder

What's the booking process?

+

1. Submit the enquiry form above with your preferred package and dates. 2. Our safari consultant contacts you within 3 hours with availability and a personalized quote. 3. If you're happy, we send a booking agreement and ask for a 20% deposit to hold your dates. 4. The balance is due 60 days before travel. That's it — we'll handle all the logistics from there.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Full refund if cancelled 60+ days before travel. 50% refund if cancelled 30-59 days before. No refund if cancelled within 30 days, but we will work to rebook your dates or issue a credit for future travel. We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance at the time of booking.

Are flights included?

+

International flights to Nairobi are not included in our packages. However, all domestic flights within Kenya (such as Nairobi to Maasai Mara) are included in the Kenya Grand Safari package. For all other packages, we can arrange domestic flights at additional cost — just let us know when you enquire.

What's the best time to visit Kenya?

+

The Great Migration in the Maasai Mara peaks July–October. For Amboseli, June–October offers the clearest Kilimanjaro views. Lake Naivasha is excellent year-round. January–February is generally excellent across all parks with fewer crowds. The long rains (April–May) bring green landscapes but some road closures and lower prices.

Do I need a visa for Kenya?

+

Most nationalities require a Kenya tourist visa (approx. $50 USD), available online at evisa.go.ke before arrival. Yellow fever vaccination is required if arriving from endemic countries. We recommend consulting your doctor about malaria prophylaxis for safari areas. Your consultant will provide a full travel preparation guide upon booking.

Is Kenya safe for families?

+

Absolutely. Thousands of families visit Kenya's safari parks every year. Our guides are trained in family safety, our vehicles are child-friendly, and our accommodation options range from family rooms to private camps. We can arrange childcare equipment, special meals, and age-appropriate activities for younger children.
